Automatic Web Sealer

High-Speed, Fully Automated Shrink Wrapping Solution for Bulk & Industrial Packaging

Model: BDX-20401 BDE-2040-A

The BDX-20401 BDE-2040-A Automatic Web Sealer & Shrink Tunnel is an advanced packaging solution ideal for bulk shrink wrapping with minimal manual intervention. Equipped with cutting-edge sealing and shrinking technology, it ensures consistent packaging quality with high-speed operation.

Applications:
  • Bulk Packaging for Beverage Bottles
  • Carton & Box Packaging
  • Food & Dairy Products
  • Stationery, Cosmetics & Pharmaceutical Products
  • Bundling of Multiple Items
Specifications:
  • Max Packing Size: L Unlimited x W 500mm x H 300mm
  • Sealing Width: 700mm
  • Packing Speed: 8-15 Packs/Minute
  • Power Supply: 380V / 50Hz, 3 Phase
  • Total Power: 20 - 24 kW
  • Film Type: PE Shrink Film
  • Machine Dimensions: Customized as per requirement
  • Control System: PLC Based fully automatic operation

This web sealer is highly suitable for industries requiring heavy-duty, continuous packaging with precise and uniform shrink results — improving both appearance and protection of your products.
