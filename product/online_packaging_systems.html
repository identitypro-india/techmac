Online Packing Systems

Streamline your end-of-line packaging with fully automated online packing solutions for continuous and efficient production.

Model: Online Packing System

The Online Packing System integrates multiple stages of packaging into a single, fully automated system, minimizing manual handling and maximizing speed and efficiency for high-volume production lines.

Applications:
  • FMCG & Consumer Goods
  • Pharmaceutical Packaging
  • Food & Beverage Industry
  • Automobile Parts & Engineering Components
  • E-commerce Fulfillment Centers
Specifications:
  • System Configuration: Carton Forming → Product Insertion → Case Sealing → Strapping → Palletizing
  • Speed: Customizable (Depends on product & production line)
  • Box Size Range: Customizable as per client requirement
  • Power Supply: 220V / 380V, 50/60 Hz
  • Control System: PLC Controlled with HMI Touchscreen
  • Integration: Can be integrated with ERP, MES, WMS systems
  • Safety Features: Emergency Stop, Light Curtains, Overload Protection, Alarms
